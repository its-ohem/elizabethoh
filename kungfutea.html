Website Audit of Kung Fu Tea


This assessment of the Kung Fu Tea website was completed as part of a Web Design class at the University of Cincinnati. Included is a website content inventory, style and presentation analysis, and critique of accessibility. As a result of lacking accessibility features and a confusing shop interface, I urge the website to implement proper design and accessibility elements so it may best serve the needs of its visitors.
